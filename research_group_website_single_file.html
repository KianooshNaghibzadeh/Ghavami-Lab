Research areas

Cancer Diagnostics

Developing translational approaches for early detection, monitoring, and characterization of cancer using advanced analytical and biomedical technologies.

Liquid Biopsy

Investigating minimally invasive biomarkers from blood and other biofluids to improve disease detection and longitudinal assessment.

Raman & SERS

Using Raman spectroscopy and surface-enhanced Raman spectroscopy to capture molecular fingerprints from cells and clinical samples.

Computational Analysis
